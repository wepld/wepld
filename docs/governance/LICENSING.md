## Coverage

- Software, documentation, evaluation fixtures, synthetic cassettes, and
  comparator files **authored in this repository** are covered by
  `Apache-2.0` unless a path explicitly declares otherwise.
- Third-party material must carry its own provenance and license
  declaration; **no third-party material is currently admitted into the
  Pre-H1 fixture registry**.
- Trademarks and branding are not licensed by Apache-2.0 beyond reasonable
  and customary use in describing the origin of the work.
- The license does not authorize use of credentials, datasets, provider
  accounts, or third-party model weights.
- Future commercial or hosted services may be delivered separately; nothing
  here constrains or promises them.

## Process boundaries

- The NOTICE file is informational and adds no restrictions altering the
  Apache-2.0 license.
- No contributor license agreement or DCO policy is introduced by this
  package.
- Any licensing change requires a separate founder/governance decision
  recorded through normal review.
