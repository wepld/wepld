# Contributing to WePLD

**WePLD is currently closed to unsolicited external contributions.**

- Pull requests, patches, or issues containing code or content from
  external parties who have not been authorized in writing are **not
  accepted** and will be closed without review of their content.
- Repository access (including read access, an invitation, or a clone)
  does **not** authorize contribution.
- Do not submit code, documentation, datasets, models, prompts, fixtures,
  or any other material to this project without prior written
  authorization from the owner.
- Unsolicited submissions confer no rights on the submitter and create no
  obligation on the project.

## Future participation

Any future contributor, contractor, or employee participation requires
written intellectual-property assignment and confidentiality terms that
have been approved by the owner and reviewed by qualified legal counsel
in the relevant jurisdiction(s) **before** any access or work begins.
No such terms are drafted or offered here — this document is a posture
statement, not a contributor license agreement.

Governing decision record: `docs/governance/decisions/GDR-006-contributor-and-contractor-policy.md`.
